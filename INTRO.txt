There is a constant need for intelligent solutions across all domains due to the rising popularity of smart cities. Smart Cities are now possible thanks to the Internet of Things' over-the-internet control function. A user only needs to use a smartphone or any other internet-connected device to operate the equipment installed in his house or office from anywhere in the globe. A smart city comprises several areas, with one of the most well-known being smart parking.

Numerous developments have been made in the smart parking sector, including the Smart Parking Management System, Smart Gate Control, Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) system, Smart Payment system, Smart Entry system, and many more.
